Pest & Disease Database

Aphids

Type: Insect Pest

Affects: Tomatoes, Peppers, Kale, Lettuce

Description: Small, soft-bodied insects that suck plant sap

Organic Solutions:

  • Spray with neem oil solution
  • Introduce ladybugs or lacewings
  • Use insecticidal soap spray
  • Plant companion plants like marigolds

Powdery Mildew

Type: Fungal Disease

Affects: Squash, Cucumbers, Zucchini

Description: White powdery growth on leaves and stems

Organic Solutions:

  • Spray with baking soda solution
  • Improve air circulation
  • Water at base of plants
  • Use milk spray (1:9 ratio with water)

Tomato Hornworm

Type: Caterpillar Pest

Affects: Tomatoes, Peppers, Eggplants

Description: Large green caterpillars that eat leaves

Organic Solutions:

  • Hand-pick caterpillars
  • Use BT (Bacillus thuringiensis) spray
  • Encourage parasitic wasps
  • Plant basil as companion

Slugs & Snails

Type: Mollusk Pest

Affects: Lettuce, Cabbage, Hostas, Seedlings

Description: Soft-bodied mollusks that chew irregular holes in leaves

Organic Solutions:

  • Set up beer traps
  • Use copper tape barriers
  • Apply diatomaceous earth around plants
  • Hand-pick in evening hours

General Prevention Tips

  • Practice crop rotation annually
  • Maintain proper plant spacing
  • Use clean, disease-free seeds
  • Water in the morning
  • Remove infected plant material
  • Encourage beneficial insects
  • Keep garden clean of debris
